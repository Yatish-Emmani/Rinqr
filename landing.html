Features

Built for the
real world.

Every feature designed around one principle: protect your privacy while staying reachable.

🔒
Complete number masking — both ways

Your real number is AES-256 encrypted and never revealed to anyone who scans your tag. The scanner's number is equally hidden from you. Both parties communicate through a Twilio relay number. Neither party can identify the other from the call alone.

Core technology
📱
No app for scanners

Zero install friction. Opens in browser instantly. More people actually use it when you need them to.

💬
Instant SMS alerts

The moment someone scans your tag you get a text with the reason. You decide if and when to call back.

🆘
Emergency escalation

Set an emergency contact. If you miss two consecutive calls from the same scan event, they're automatically notified.

🌧️
Built to survive anything

BOPP vinyl with gloss laminate. UV-resistant, waterproof, car-wash safe. QR level H — reads even when damaged.

📊
Full scan history

Every scan logged with timestamp, reason, and approximate location. See patterns, export your data.
