Features

BUILT FOR THE
REAL WORLD.

Every feature designed around one principle: protect your privacy while staying reachable.

🔒
Complete number masking — both ways

Your real number is AES-256 encrypted and never revealed to anyone who scans your tag. The scanner's number is equally hidden from you. Both parties communicate through a Twilio relay number. Neither party can identify the other from the call alone.

Core technology
📱
No app for scanners

The person who scans your tag needs zero app install. It opens in their browser. Instant. No friction means more people actually use it when you need them to.

💬
Instant SMS alerts

The moment someone scans your tag you get a text with the reason they're reaching out. You decide if and when to call back.

🆘
Emergency escalation

Set an emergency contact. If you miss two consecutive calls from the same scan event, your contact is automatically notified.

🌧️
Built to survive anything

BOPP vinyl with gloss laminate. UV-resistant, waterproof, car-wash safe. QR error correction level H means it still scans even if 30% of it is damaged.

📊
Full scan history

Every scan logged with timestamp, reason, and approximate location. See patterns, export your data, and manage multiple tags from one dashboard.
