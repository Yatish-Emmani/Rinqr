01 — Who we are

WHO WE ARE

Rinqr ("Rinqr," "we," "us," or "our") is a privacy-first vehicle contact platform. Our service allows vehicle owners to be contacted anonymously via a QR tag — without their phone number, identity, or personal information being revealed to the person contacting them.

This Privacy Policy governs all data collected through our website at rinqr.com and any associated subdomains, our web application, our physical QR tags, and any related services (collectively, the "Service").

Questions? Contact us at privacy@rinqr.com

03 — How we collect

HOW WE COLLECT IT

Information you provide directly

When you activate a Rinqr tag, you enter your phone number and verify it via a one-time passcode (OTP). This is the primary data collection event. You may also optionally provide an email address to create a dashboard account.

Information collected when a tag is scanned

When someone scans your QR tag, we collect the scan timestamp, the reason selected, and the phone number they enter. We also collect an approximate location based on their IP address — city-level precision only, not GPS. This lets you know roughly where your car was when it was scanned.

Information from third-party services

We use Twilio to power our anonymous phone relay. When a scan event triggers a call or SMS, Twilio processes the phone numbers involved. We share only the minimum necessary information with Twilio to complete the relay. See Section 13 for full details.

04 — Why we use it

WHY WE USE YOUR DATA

To deliver the core service

Legal basis: Contract performance. We need your phone number to route anonymous calls and SMS alerts to you. Without this, the Service cannot function.

To verify your identity during activation

Legal basis: Contract performance + fraud prevention. We send a one-time passcode to confirm you control the phone number you're registering.

To send you scan alerts

Legal basis: Contract performance. We send you an SMS alert each time your tag is scanned. This is not marketing — it is the Service itself.

To log scan history (Driver plan and above)

Legal basis: Contract performance. Paid plan subscribers have access to a full scan history dashboard. You can export or delete this data at any time.

To operate and improve the Service

Legal basis: Legitimate interest. We use anonymized, aggregated usage data to understand how the Service is used and diagnose technical issues. No individual is identifiable from this data.

To comply with legal obligations

Legal basis: Legal obligation. We may retain certain data to comply with applicable law or respond to lawful requests from law enforcement. We will notify you of any such request unless prohibited by law.

What we never do with your data: We do not serve you advertisements. We do not sell your data. We do not build behavioral profiles. We do not share your phone number with the person who scanned your tag — ever, under any circumstance.
05 — Protection

HOW WE PROTECT YOUR DATA

Encryption at rest

All phone numbers stored in our database are encrypted using AES-256. Encryption keys are stored separately from the data and rotated regularly. Even if our database were compromised, phone numbers would not be readable without the keys.

Encryption in transit

All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher. We enforce HTTPS across all pages and API endpoints.

Access controls

Access to production systems and customer data is restricted to essential personnel only. All administrative access requires multi-factor authentication.

The relay design is itself a privacy protection

Our privacy architecture is structural, not just policy-based. When a call is relayed through Twilio, neither party receives the other's real number from the call metadata. This is not a setting we can accidentally turn off — it is how the system is built.

Breach notification

In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you by SMS or email within 72 hours of becoming aware, consistent with GDPR Article 33 and CCPA breach notification requirements.

No system is perfectly secure. We implement industry-standard protections but cannot guarantee absolute security. Contact us immediately at security@rinqr.com if you suspect unauthorized use of your account.

CALIFORNIA RESIDENTS

If you are a California resident, the CCPA as amended by the CPRA grants you additional rights:

You have the right to know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell (we do not sell). You may request this for the 12 months preceding your request.

Right to delete

You have the right to request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions where retention is required by law.

Right to opt out of sale

We do not sell personal information. There is nothing to opt out of. This is a standing policy, not a toggleable setting.

Right to non-discrimination

We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights. It will not affect your access, pricing, or quality of service.

To submit a CCPA request: privacy@rinqr.com with subject "CCPA Request."

The Service is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use the Service.
