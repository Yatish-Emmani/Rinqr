02 — Service

DESCRIPTION OF SERVICE

Rinqr provides a privacy-first vehicle contact platform. Tag owners attach a QR sticker to their vehicle and register a phone number with the Service. When someone scans the QR code, Rinqr routes communication between the scanner and the tag owner via an anonymous relay — neither party's real phone number is revealed to the other.

The Service includes: anonymous call and SMS relay, scan activity dashboard, scan history, emergency contact escalation, and a property manager portal for business customers.
